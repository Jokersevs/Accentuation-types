The methodology we created
The structural-characterological personality study ‘Study of character in the personality structure’ (SCPS) is the result of many years of testing on the basis of such well-known psychological instruments for the study of individual-typological characteristics as: the methodology ‘Determination of character accentuations’ (K. Leonhard and S. Schmiszek), the MINI-MULT questionnaire (a short version of the Minnesota Multidimensional Personality Inventory MMPI, adapted by F. Berezin and M. Miroshnikov), and the Personality Self-portrait (J. Oldham and L. Morris).

Persistent-distrustful type

Representatives of this type are the first in terms of purposefulness. They are extroverts and active politicians, they know well how to work with the masses, how and where to lead them. People of this type are characterized by communication from the position of a senior, a tendency to teach. As employees, they strive to achieve high performance in any sphere, having increased demands on both others and themselves. They are sensitive to social injustice, and can pursue justice, at the same time they are suspicious, distrustful, offensive, sometimes extremely self-confident, jealous. They are characterized by getting stuck (fixed) on the thoughts, feelings, ideas, especially when they have a social orientation. At the same time they cannot forget the insult, being intransigent, prone to initiating conflicts, revealing a love of power. Personalities of this type are independent, consult exclusively with themselves, easily make decisions, take responsibility and can take care of themselves. They take criticism very seriously and do not evaluate it as intimidation. They work intensively to deserve a good attitude in the team. They are careful in working relationships, rely on devotion, loyalty to them, and try to learn as much as possible about persons before contacting them. They have specific abilities for perception, quickly notice hidden messages, or attempts to mislead them, which makes them dangerous rivals, especially in business relationships.

Authoritarian type

People of this type work best in such power structures where everyone knows their place, and everyone understands their powers. They are purposeful, have a pragmatic approach to achieving their goals. They do everything to get the desired result. Such individuals are physically strong and often take part in sports competitions, especially where there is close contact with the opponent. They have reduced contact in communication, reveal inhibition of verbal and non-verbal reactions. In an emotionally calm state, they are conscientious, neat, even pedantic, love animals and children. Under adverse conditions, they can be boring, gloomy, prone to rudeness, quarrels. During excitement, they have poor control over their behavior; under the influence of stress factors they reveal impulsiveness, weakening of control over aspirations, a tendency to conflicts, which leads to misunderstandings and even to despotism and cruelty in the working team and in the family. But apart from fits of anger and irritation, this type is characterized by conscientiousness, neatness, perfectionism. Such people have a high opinion of themselves when disagreement is perceived as a personal insult. They are not flexible, have difficulty adapting to changes, are conservative, too straightforward, often suspect people of insincerity, which is why they tend to hurt and offend loved ones, and they consider being the first to reconcile to be a humiliation of their own dignity. This creates additional problems in relationships. However, a person of this type can cope well with analytical activities: in any dispute or interaction, they play the role of a leading analyst.

Demonstrative type

People of this type are given a wonderful gift with which they decorate the lives of others, because for a representative of this type the whole world is a theater, life is never sad or boring, including close friends. That is why they are also called the dramatic type. They are demonstrative, emotional and physically tender, quickly react to all events, being expansive, lively, bright. They have a rich imagination, can tell interesting stories, love romances and melodramas. They want to be singled out and noticed to be in the focus of attention. They pay a lot of attention to their appearance and enjoy their clothes, style, image. They are seductive, sexy, charming and temperamental. They quickly get involved in relationships, easily supporting new ideas and suggestions from others. They are characterized by the ease of establishing contacts, the desire for power, leadership, praise. Therefore, they adapt well to people, have a tendency to intrigue, sometimes annoy others with their self-confidence and provoking conflicts. Positive qualities: gallantry, artistry, the ability to captivate others, unusual thinking and actions. Negative qualities: selfishness, hypocrisy, boastfulness, a pronounced desire to be the center of attention and achieve their goals at any cost: with tears, clothes, unusual hobbies. For this type, others’ opinion is important. People of the demonstrative type are emotional and often act without thinking, and planning is not their strong point. In work, they are prone to diversity, but they do not cope well with painstaking work that requires concentration on details. The demonstrative type of personality is adaptive, easily adapts to new situations, and tends not to dwell on the past.

Hyperthymic type

Hyperthymic persons stand out among others with an increased level of mood and optimism. They are leaders, bright lights, the center of attention of society and family, confident in their abilities and open to their possibilities. They always expect only good treatment from others, they can take advantage of the strengths and abilities of other people to achieve goals. They are emotionally oriented, well, deeply understand their own feelings, thoughts and inner state. They highly value and protect comfort, free time and the joys of life. No one can take away the right to personal freedom from such a person: if something threatens their personal freedom, they energetically defend their right to do what they want. These individuals avoid any exploitation, they resist demands that they consider unreasonable or above their strength. They are cheerful and optimistic if meet with reasonable demands. People of this type are characterized by sociability, brightness of facial expressions and gestures. They often deviate from the topic in conversation. They have conflicts with others about negligence and frivolous attitude towards official and family duties, they can be offended when they are made remarks. Positive qualities: energy, initiative, optimism, thirst for activity, their mood is always good. Negative qualities: tendency to ill-considered actions, increased irritability, frivolous attitude towards their duties. They have difficulty in enduring discipline, monotonous activity, loneliness. In childhood and adolescence, they had many different hobbies, but these hobbies, as a rule, are superficial and quickly pass. According to our observations, they are prone to travel, long journeys, can tolerate adverse living conditions.

Withdrawn-introverted type

Such individuals are very different from others. They are dreamers, great originals and often differ from others in their eccentricity, and sometimes in their genius. They are tuned in to themselves and support their own dreams, it does not matter to them whether others accept their attitude to life. They are self-oriented and independent, need little closeness in relationships, they are characterized by little friendliness. They are characterized by abstract reflections in solitude, therefore they do not need anyone but themselves, being free from emotions and entertainment, which can be annoying to other people. They are inferior to others in sensitivity, in close relationships; the area in which they excel is observation: they know how to reveal such facts in life that others do not notice. These people are calm, dispassionate and unsentimental. They demonstrate indifference to pain and pleasure. They are not bothered by criticism, they are also indifferent to praise, they themselves evaluate their behavior. Positive qualities: they are reserved, have strong beliefs, principled. Negative qualities: they poorly understand the states and experiences of other people, they do not know how to empathize, their inner world is most often filled with various fantasies, special hobbies. In the external manifestation of their feelings, they are quite reserved, not always understood by other people who, as a rule, do not like them very much.

Conscientious-pedantic type

People of this type reveal high moral principles, being very responsible, they do not allow themselves to rest until the work is done and done correctly. They are devoted to their families, their work, their leaders. They rarely enter into conflicts, being at the passive side. In the service, they make too high demands on others, but are inferior in leadership. Positive qualities: conscientiousness, accuracy, seriousness, reliability in business and feelings. Negative qualities: formalism, pedantry, excessive scrupulousness. People of this type are characterized by early intellectual development, a tendency to reasoning, introspection and assessments of other people's behavior. Most anxious people belong to this type: they are worried that they may not meet the requirements of management, colleagues, loved ones. These people may feel ashamed for others, they are worried about the "imperfectness" of the world. Due to their pronounced perfectionism, people with this personality type may suffer from psychosomatic illnesses.

Emotional type

They prefer to communicate in a narrow circle of people with whom they have established good relations, understand them "at a glance". They rarely enter into conflicts. They carry resentments within themselves, have deep reactions in the sphere of subtle emotions, humanity, sensitivity. Such people deeply experience the slightest troubles, are too sensitive to remarks, failures, are non-conflict type. They, being altruists and good family men, rejoice in other people's successes, have a high sense of duty. They believe that life is for serving, love is for giving. They know how to empathize, love children, animals, help the weak. Being non-ambitious, they are accessible in relations with others. People of this type are always excellent in business relations, they are honest, ethical, sociable, do not require a lot of attention. Positive qualities: kindness, empathy, sense of duty, diligence, thoroughness. Negative qualities: excessive sensitivity, tearfulness, insecurity, indecision. They are very naive and do not realize what a profound impact they have on the lives of others, to whom they give themselves completely.

Dysthymic type

People of this type do not know how to look at everything from the positive side, they are more often pessimists than optimists. Their strength lies in the fact that they can help everyone survive in difficult times, as they maintain a "sober" view of things in various circumstances, including extreme ones. They realistically assess their own abilities, are also aware of their shortcomings and do not have inflated self-esteem. Taking responsibility for their actions, they analyze, evaluate, and think about everything before taking any action. They criticize themselves and others, always expecting the worst results, and if everything happens that way, they are already prepared for it. People of this type are characterized by low contact, a pessimistic mood. Such people rarely enter into conflicts, revealing a closed lifestyle. They highly value friends and are ready to obey them. Positive qualities: seriousness, a heightened sense of justice, and conscientiousness. They are non-conflict and at the same time serious, highly moral, conscientious, fair. Negative qualities: passivity, inhibited thinking, individualism. constantly low mood and activity, sadness, taciturnity, pessimism. Such people consider noisy companies to be burdensome for themselves, they do not get along well with their colleagues.

Conformal type

People of this personality type clearly follow their leadership, they are communicative, talkative, and respect authority. In order not to disturb the harmony in relationships and order, they are always polite, courteous, and tactful. They consider it wiser to obey, and they accept orders from their superiors without hesitation. Both among friends and in the family, they yield leadership to another. This type demonstrates uncritical attitude, and often are opportunistic, revealing submission to any authority, the majority in the group/team. They are inclined to conservatism, and their main life credo is “to be like everyone.” We can rarely meet such people in a dispute – they do not like to be in the center of other people’s attention. If they find themselves involved in a controversial situation, they remain passive onlookers, observing events from afar. They being of the non-conflict type are more comfortable following the crowd, rather than defending their own opinion/interests or the opinion of the minority. However, no one but them will cope better with finding a compromise in a dispute. Positive qualities: they are characterized by great friendliness, ready to listen to the "confession" of others, diligent, non-conflict. Negative qualities: as a rule, they do not have their own opinion, very dependent, disorganized, inconsistent in their actions and cannon see the prospects of their life.

Anxious type

People of an anxious type, as a rule, have a low mood, they fear for themselves and their loved ones. They are vulnerable and constantly feels their inferiority. But, as a rule, they are friendly, self-critical, and meticulous. Friendly relationships, comfort, and coziness are typical for this type of individuality. The imagination of the anxious type people and the spirit of inquiry are well developed in them, but they are very concerned about other people opinion. In relationships, they show care, behave very tactfully, limiting themselves in their actions. Anxious people are in no hurry to tell others their thoughts and feelings. They are characterized by low contact, some cowardice, insecurity, and a low mood. They rarely enter into conflicts. Positive qualities: sociability, self-criticism, conscientiousness, a sense of duty, high moral demands on themselves and others. Negative qualities: this type is characterized by increased suspiciousness and capriciousness, fatigue and irritability, especially when performing a difficult task. A tendency to hypochondria is their particularly typical trait. They are characterized by inferiority complex revealing some difficulties in adapting to a new team, difficult living conditions. They often compensate for shortcomings in their character traits by choosing difficult activities and increased diligence.

Unstable type

People of this personality type have their own value system, love risk and usually take part in the most dangerous activities. They are not very concerned about the problems of others and believe that everyone can take care of themselves, do not feel guilty about the past and do not worry about the future, as they live one day at a time. Planning their actions is not characteristic of them, they act according to the situation and rather intuitively. It is important that their opinion is valued. They are prone to conflicts, if they are wrong, they prefer to blame others. Positive qualities: they love to travel, go on a trip in order to walk, explore, observe new environments. People with this personality type are usually brave, very energetic, love risky actions, which can help them perform dangerous activities in extreme conditions. They are courageous, physically strong and durable, can lead when others are lost. Negative qualities: they show an increased tendency and attraction to entertainment, as well as to idleness and laziness. It is difficult to predict the behavior of this type of personality. They are impulsive, self-control is difficult for them. They can show themselves aggressively, ignoring generally accepted norms of behavior in society

Labile type

People of this type are active, passionate in their desire to connect/interact with life and with other people. Everything that happens between them and other people cannot be perceived easily by them. They have vivid emotions, high sensitivity to external phenomena, talkativeness, love, high contact. At the same time, such people are altruistic, compassionate, reveal artistic taste, are attached to friends. This type is distinguished by energy, such people are always busy, creative, active, enthusiastic. They show initiative and can also charge others with their activity, being constantly in search of something new. They argue very often, but do not lead to conflicts. They are characterized by frequent changes in mood, communication manners. They have a hard time experiencing minor troubles, react extremely irritable to the remarks of others, but can also be passionately happy about something, are exalted. Positive qualities: attentive to loved ones and relatives; altruistic, empathetic; have good taste; show vivid feelings. Negative qualities: this type is changeable in mood, and it is most often unpredictable, and the reasons for an unexpected change in mood may be insignificant. According to such state, the present and future for them can be colored either in rainbow or dark colors. When people of this type are in a depressed mood, they need help and support from loved ones who are able to distract, encourage and entertain them.
